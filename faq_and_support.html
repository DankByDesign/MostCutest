General Questions

Is Lil Brother Games safe and secure?

Absolutely! We take all precautions possible to protect our youngest users. We transport users through encrypted web sockets through a relay transport to avoid needing to work around firewalls and be cross platform since you are fully secured! We work with top payment companies & app stores, which guarantees your safety and security. All billing information is stored securely by them and only passed through transit by us.

What platforms is Most Cutest on?

Most Cutest is released on both IOS and Android on the google play store and apple app store. Soon to be localized for other countries and app stores as well through the unity distribution portal.

Is Most Cutest Cross Platform?

Yes and thanks to our single sign on service being fulfilled through Unity PLayer Accounts you can have one account shared over multiple platforms for the ultimate cross platform experience.

Can I request data deletion

Yes and through two clicks on the app.

h3>Can I delete my card if it went to print in your physical card game?

Yes you can delete it from our servers and user generated content service but you cannot delete it from our physical card game. By agreeing to our terms of service and purchasing a blank card you grant us license to do so.

Is my data collected as an anonymous user?

No with the execption of an identifier used by unity authentication for anonymous users for a limited experience in our app.

Can I be a moderator?

We moderate cards ourselves before they become publicly available and do not seek the moderation of others.

Can I report a player or their content?

We moderate cards ourselves and filter for malicious usernames ourselves. So no.
